Utility Test

Specific Problem Required
02

Longevity Test

Will this tool still be relevant in 2 years? Tools built on shaky business models, dying markets or trend-dependent demand are excluded.

Long-term Viability Required
03

ROI Test

Does the measurable output justify the cost? Every dollar spent on a tool must return more than a dollar in value — time, money, or both.

Positive ROI Required
